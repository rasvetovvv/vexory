# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Digital builders: founders, developers, designers, PMs, marketers, AI engineers, investors, freelancers. They arrive in work mode — checking progress on projects they follow, posting a build-log update, scouting for teammates or roles. Sessions are frequent and short; the interface must disappear into the task.

## Product Purpose

Vexory is a project-first social network. The core entity is the Project (not the person, not the post): every project has a page with a build log, roadmap, team and open roles, and all feed activity derives from real product work. Success = builders shipping in public, forming teams through role applications, and followers tracking genuine progress instead of noise.

## Brand Personality

Focused, ambitious, quietly premium. "Signal, not noise" — quality networking without memes or filler. The purple liquid-glass aesthetic conveys a high-end night-time workspace: deep, calm, luminous where it matters.

## Anti-references

- LinkedIn: resume-first profiles, engagement-bait feed, corporate beige.
- Generic SaaS dashboards: cream backgrounds, hero-metric cards, identical card grids.
- Crypto/web3 landing pages: aggressive neon, gradient text, decorative glassmorphism on everything.
- The earlier green "Matrix terminal" direction (refer/project/DESIGN.md) was explicitly rejected — purple glass is the identity.

## Design Principles

1. **The project is the hero.** Layout and hierarchy always foreground project state (status, progress, latest update) over vanity metrics.
2. **Glass is material, not decoration.** Translucency and blur mark elevated layers (panels, overlays, sticky chrome); flat surfaces stay flat.
3. **Activity reads like a log.** Timestamps, statuses and roles use the mono style; human sentences use the sans. That contrast is the voice.
4. **Earned familiarity.** Standard affordances (tabs, chips, forms) done precisely; no invented controls.
5. **Dark that stays clean.** Ambient color lives in large, smooth, low-chroma fields; no banding, no blotchy glows competing with content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Dark theme only for now. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 against surfaces; muted/metadata text ≥ 3:1 minimum at small sizes. All interactive states (hover/focus/disabled) visible; focus rings on keyboard navigation. Respect prefers-reduced-motion for any non-trivial animation.
